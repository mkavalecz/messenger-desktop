Everything you need, nothing you don't

🔔

Unread badge (with sound)

A red dot appears on the tray icon whenever you have unread messages, and you get the official sound effect as well.

📌

System tray

Minimize to tray, close to tray, start minimized, all optional. You can use any combination, just the way you like it.

🔐

Persistent session

Your login session and the window position are saved between restarts so you can get back to chatting instantly.

🔄

Update check

Option to check for new releases on startup. Get a desktop notification for updates — no auto-installs, no surprises.

📹

Voice & video calls

Full support for voice and video calls — straight from your desktop. Even if your default browser is not based on Chrome.

🔗

Your browser, your choice

Links open in whichever browser you prefer — not the one your OS decided for you. No more vendor lock-in.
